About us

Who We Are

Capture You is a nonprofit connecting photographers and clients throughout the greater Seattle area. In a world where most people find their photographers through only word-of-mouth, everyone is met with extremely limited options. We bring a powerful tool into this equation. Our state of the art search and booking features empower clients to quickly find their ideal photographer. While our portfolio and client management features allow photographers to grow their business. For all of our customers, we provide simple, instant connections.

Our Story

On a more personal level, we’re a group of five, highly-driven students based out of the University of Washington. Brought together by a class, we quickly built a team with a diverse skill set and set out to improve our local community. Despite our varied personalities, we all share a passion for art, and an appreciation for the difficulties associated with growing an artistic business. Because of this, we aimed our focus towards aiding photographers. Now that we’re off and running, our day to day revolves around answering one simple question, “What can we do for the local artist?”
